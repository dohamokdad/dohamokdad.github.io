Our technology platforms combine the stable base of an autonomous robot with sensor, communication, and interaction packages customized to match our customer’s needs.

Our robots are:

  • Easily deployed by just one person
  • Highly maneuverable on land or through water
  • Fully autonomous using onboard vision and inertial sensors
  • Extremely programmable and highly configurable
  • Lightweight and portable, our robots can travel as checked luggage

AQUA robots have been deployed successfully in freshwater lakes, rivers, the Pacific, the Atlantic, and with extensive time spent mapping coral reefs in the Caribbean.
