Robots and Services

Our AQUA robots are amphibious, autonomous and equipped with vision and sensor platforms for shore, shallows and mid-depth exploration.

The custom software solutions that we build and deliver all use ROS as a control architecture and all custom hardware is equipped with rugged underwater enclosures.

The rugged waterproof legged mobility platform uses patented applications and requires no computing infrastructure.

Our vision-based swimming robots have the capability for a variety of autonomous underwater behaviours and all include:

  • Dual stack control system
  • Multiple-camera vision system
  • Multi-layered autonomy

AQUA

AQUA is the ideal platform for submersible robotics projects, environmental monitoring, and propulsion research.
The robot is highly programmable and configurable and is capable of propelling itself on land or through water.
The use of six independently controlled thrusting surfaces makes the unit highly maneuverable and produces minimal disturbance to the surrounding environment.
